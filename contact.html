Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our printing services

What file formats do you accept?

We accept PDF, JPG, PNG, AI, PSD, CDR, and most other common design file formats. For best results, we recommend high-resolution PDF files.

How long does printing usually take?

Standard printing jobs take 24-48 hours. Rush orders can be completed within a few hours depending on quantity and complexity. Large orders may take 3-5 days.

Do you provide design services?

Yes! Our expert designers can help create or modify your designs. Design consultation is free, and we charge separately for custom design work.

What are your minimum order quantities?

Most digital printing has no minimum order. Screen printing requires minimum 25 pieces. Offset printing minimum is 100 pieces.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery within Mumbai for orders above ₹500. Pickup is also available from our Dadar East location during business hours.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI, credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. For large orders, we may require 50% advance payment.
